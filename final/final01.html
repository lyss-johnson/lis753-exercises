Fox Facts and Information

Introduction

Foxes are mammals that are light on their feet. They have long, thin legs, lithe frame, pointed nose and bushy tail. Foxes are social and live flexible lives. They are found all over the world — in North America, Europe, Asia and North Africa — and call a wide range of terrains their home. They also eat a greatly varied diet.

Quick Facts

Habitat
- Foxes are extremely versatile when it comes to the environment where they live. They live all around the world throughout the Northern Hemisphere.
Food
- Foxes are omnivores and will consume just about anything.
Antonmy
- Foxes are build for strength and speed. They are very slender animals with a long nose and pointed ears.
